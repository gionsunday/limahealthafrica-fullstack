Home Sample Collection

For patient convenience, we offer home sample collection services, allowing individuals to access essential laboratory tests without leaving their homes. Our trained technicians ensure safe and hygienic sample collection, adhering to stringent standards to provide a seamless experience and minimize travel and wait times for patients.

Online Result Review:

To make results more accessible and convenient, we offer secure online result review options. Patients and healthcare providers can quickly access and review lab results through a secure online portal, ensuring privacy and enabling prompt medical consultation and follow-up when needed.

At Lima Health Africa, our laboratory services are designed to make quality healthcare more accessible, ensuring patients receive timely and accurate diagnostics wherever they are. Through a commitment to advanced technology and personalized care, we help empower patients and providers alike with the essential information they need for informed health decisions.
